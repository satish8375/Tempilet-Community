🎨 Tempilet Community Templates
Welcome to the Tempilet Community! Here you can share your designs with the world.

🚀 How to Publish Your Template:
Open Tempilet App and finish your design.
Export your design for Community (Prepare for GitHub). You will get a .json and a .png file.
Come to this repository and open the templates/ folder.
Click Add file -> Upload files and drop your .json file.
Go to the previews/ folder and upload your .png file.
Important: After uploading, wait for the admin to update the templates.csv file, or if you know how, edit it yourself!
📢 Contribution Rules:
No copyright-protected images.
Keep the design clean and useful for others.
Only upload original content created by you.
